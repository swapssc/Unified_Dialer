About UVC

Unified Voice is a globally recognized VoIP service and solutions provider.

It helps organizations to implement end-to-end IP Based solutions on varied technology platforms. We are operational in India since year 2003 with an objective to cater to the upcoming needs of the emerging global telecom markets. Today Unified Voice has an impressive list of over 1000+ corporate clients spread across the country. It's been a constant endeavor of Voicecom always, to bring the latest technology in VOIP to the market so as to offer its customers upcoming and innovative services and solutions in the telecom domain.
